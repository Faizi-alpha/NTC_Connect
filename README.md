<b>NTC Touch </b>  is the legitimate <b>NTC Mobile Android app </b> that ambitions to behave as a companion app for the people of Nitra Technical Campus,Ghaziabad. The app consists of numerous useful modules that allow students to live up-to-date with all the college information, keep a time table in keeping with their timetable, discover all approximately exciting locations in and round university, and plenty of extra (NTC Stands for Nitra Technical Campus. Its an engineering institute located in Ghaziabad).
But this assignment is a lot extra than an app. This is an <b>initiative</b> this is targeted at all of the emerging developers and encourages them to participate in this as a collaborative open-source team effort. So in case you are interested and you adore growing programs and want to peer them used by a large number of human beings, you may make contributions to the application and might even emerge as a moderator of it.
